5Ô∏è‚É£ Read Alignment with STAR, HISAT2 & Minimap2 Link to heading

üß≠ Align your reads to a reference genome.

# HISAT2 (alternative short-read aligner)

# Minimap2 (for long reads)

8Ô∏è‚É£ Automating Pipelines with Snakemake Link to heading
